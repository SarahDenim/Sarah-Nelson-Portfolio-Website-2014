News Bubble Application


I worked in a team on creating a virtual reality news application for Android phones to be used with Google Cardboard. The aim for the project was to create something that would bring journalism into the 21st century. My group came up with the idea of a virtual world map, overlaid with news stories and other information, in order to provide a global perspective on what's happening in the world. We wanted to get away from the trend of over-personalisation and allow users to recieve the same information as every other user.


My group after we were presented with the award for "Best Social and Mobile" project at the showcase for the Social and Mobile Computing course at UQ.
